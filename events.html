Event Descriptions

Day 1: Crisis Management

This dynamic speaking exercise challenges participants to deliver a concise talk on crisis management within just a minute. It tests quick thinking, clear articulation, and composure under pressure—crucial skills for managing real-life crises.

Day 1: Business Trivia

Quiz or game that tests participants' knowledge about various aspects of the business world. It encompasses a wide range of topics, including historical events, key figures, financial terms, company facts, and industry-specific knowledge.

Day 2: Market Whiz: Sell it like a pro

Participants will be given a product and tasked with gathering real-time feedback and insights from fellow students on campus. They’ll need to analyze the data, identify trends, and present their findings creatively within a limited time. It’s a race against the clock to uncover valuable consumer insights and showcase their market research skills!
