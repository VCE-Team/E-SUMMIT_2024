Event Descriptions

Day 1: Crisis Management

This dynamic speaking exercise challenges participants to deliver a concise talk on crisis management within just a minute. It tests quick thinking, clear articulation, and composure under pressure—crucial skills for managing real-life crises.

Day 1: Business Trivia

A quiz that tests participants' knowledge about the business world, covering topics such as historical events, key figures, financial terms, and industry-specific knowledge.

Day 2: Rapid Fire Quiz

A fast-paced event that tests participants' knowledge about startups, entrepreneurship, and innovation. It encourages quick thinking and recalling key facts and trends from the entrepreneurial world.

Day 2: Market Whiz: Sell it like a pro

Participants gather real-time feedback on a product from fellow students, analyze the data, and present their findings creatively within a limited time. It's a race against the clock to uncover consumer insights and showcase market research skills!
